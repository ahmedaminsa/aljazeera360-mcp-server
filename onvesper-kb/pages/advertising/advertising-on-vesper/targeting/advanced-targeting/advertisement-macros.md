# Advertisement Macros

Advertisement macros are variables used within an ad tag. They are designed to be dynamically replaced with specific values when the ad is served and are a key element of targeted advertising as well as collection of data relevant to the ad's performance.

All macros must be appended to the ad tag and added as key-value pairs on the Supply Side Platform of your choice.

Depending on your campaign targeting strategy and the consent laws prevalent in the area(s) where your service operates, Endeavor offers a wide range of macros for you to choose from. Note that some Macro values will not be returned if you do not offer a Consent Management Platform (CMP) and have captured appropriate personalisation consent from your consumer, these are indicated below.
