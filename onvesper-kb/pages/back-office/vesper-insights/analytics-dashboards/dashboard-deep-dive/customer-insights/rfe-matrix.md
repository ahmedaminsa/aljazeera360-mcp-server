# RFE Matrix

<figure><img src="/files/NKYD9Kwn159gSpxGs498" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Use Cases

This matrix  captures the distribution of customer activity based on recency, frequency, and engagement levels.&#x20;

It can be utilised to identify patterns, trends, and correlations between recency of engagement, frequency of content consumption, and the extent of engagement in terms of viewing hours.

This matrix serves as a valuable and targeted analytical resource for identifying trends, optimising content recommendations, and tailoring engagement strategies to enhance user satisfaction and retention.
