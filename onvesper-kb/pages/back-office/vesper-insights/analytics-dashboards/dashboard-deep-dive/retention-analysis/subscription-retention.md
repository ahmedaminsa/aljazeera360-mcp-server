### Use Cases

A subscription retention dashboard serves as a crucial tool for businesses operating on a subscription model, offering a comprehensive overview of subscriber retention metrics.

By segmenting customers based on various criteria, the dashboard allows you to tailor retention strategies to specific subscriber groups.Through a subscription retention dashboard, businesses gain insights into the effectiveness of their retention initiatives, allowing for informed decision-making. Ultimately, the subscription retention dashboard plays a pivotal role in optimizing revenue, enhancing customer loyalty, and ensuring the long-term viability of subscription-based business models.
