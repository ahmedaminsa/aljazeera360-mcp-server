### Use Cases

Using this dashboard, you will be able to see what percentage of customers convert their free trial subscriptions into paid licences.&#x20;

This information provides valuable insights into the effectiveness of the free trial offering and the ability to convert potential customers into paying customers. By tracking the free trial conversion rate, you can gauge the success of their marketing and sales strategies.&#x20;

A high conversion rate indicates that the free trial is attracting interested customers who are willing to convert into paying subscribers, demonstrating the value and appeal of the product or service.
