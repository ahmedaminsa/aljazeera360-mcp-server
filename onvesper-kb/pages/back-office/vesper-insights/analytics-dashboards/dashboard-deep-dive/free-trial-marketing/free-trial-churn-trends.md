### Use Cases

It is important to see how many free trial licenses churned because it provides insights into the effectiveness of their trial-to-paid conversion process. Churned free trial licenses indicate customers who canceled their trial and did not convert to a paid subscription. This metric helps you understand the success of the trial offerings and identify potential areas for improvement.

Knowing the specific day when customers canceled their free trial subscription is necessary because it allows you to analyse the time frame during which customers are most likely to churn. This information can help them identify patterns or trends that may influence customer behavior and make informed decisions to optimise their conversion strategies.
