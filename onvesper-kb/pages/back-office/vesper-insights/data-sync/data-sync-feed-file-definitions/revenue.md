# Revenue

The revenue offers a comprehensive overview of payment transactions, encompassing customers' license purchases, renewals, and refunds, including the amounts reimbursed to customers. All references here of transaction is related to a financial transaction and is associated to some order placed in the system. The revenue data makes every attempt to carry with it fields from the customer licence so that there is sufficient information to attribute the revenue to, such as order id, the country the order was placed, etc.

The revenue contains the following information:

* **WHO:** Identifies the customer associated with a specific transaction.
* **WHEN:** Indicates the date and time when the transaction occurred.
* **WHAT:** Describes the license the customer purchased, including its cost, currency used for payment, and other relevant details.
* **HOW:** Specifies the payment provider, device, or other means utilized by the customer to purchase or renew the license.

While revenue records exist for IAP payments such as Apple, Google, Roku, etc. (ie: payments not managed by Endeavor Streaming), the revenue should not be relied on. For accurate revenue reporting of IAP(non-managed payments) please contact the app store for their payment collection reporting.

### FAQ

This section attempts to answer the most frequent questions about data you find in this revenue data sync feed

1. Why are amount fields 0.00 or not available for many of the IAP payment providers?
   * The receipts we receive from the IAP store do not provide it. For true revenue reporting of IAP purchases, we recommend you retrieve that data from the source of truth which are the app stores in this case.
   * Even though some IAP may have values for revenue, use it as a guide. It is still expected and recommended that you retrieve that data from the source of truth which are the app stores in this case.
2. We see a lot of revenue records for IAP payment\_provider where the record says revenue is\_pending=true with no settlement\_date and they never settle, ie: is\_pending does not change to false
   * These are unconfirmed purchase order requests received for what the platform could not verify with the app store. These are for customer\_licence orders that did not successfully convert, in other words is\_successfully\_converted=false. And as such are recorded as revenue records that never settled and corresponding licence not converted to paid.
