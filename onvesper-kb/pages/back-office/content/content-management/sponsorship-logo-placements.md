# Sponsorship Logo Placements

## Web Application

Incorporate an on-brand image for your sponsorship partner on your web app.

### Option 1: Branded Hero Image

Create a hero slide of your sponsorship partner. Background image to be the sponsorship imagery provided by the sponsor *(Recommended size: 1920x1080, no more than 1MB)* with a CTA button pointing to the live event or VOD content sponsored by the partner.&#x20;

{% code overflow="wrap" %}

```
How to: Vesper Backoffice -> Content -> Content Manager -> Hero -> Add a new slide -> Add sponsorship imagery as background image 
```

{% endcode %}

### Option 2: Branded Hero Image with Logo and Title Image &#x20;

Add a sponsorship logo to a hero image and include a CTA button to drive end users to native section, playlist, video, or third party site.&#x20;

* This can be a sponsorship logo baked into the background image or;
* Utilize the [title image option ](/platform-knowledge-base/back-office/content/content-management/creating-hero-images.md)to upload a transparent png logo of your sponsorship partner.
  * *Recommended size for title image 640x480 - PNG transparency.*&#x20;

<figure><img src="/files/oeT40gECHIBlyVe8gJln" alt=""><figcaption><p>"The Night Manager" is a title image that can be used for a sponsorship logo rather than plain text</p></figcaption></figure>

{% code overflow="wrap" %}

```
How to: Vesper Backoffice -> Content -> Content Manager -> Hero -> Add a new slide -> Add sponsorship imagery as background image 

Add title image: Vesper Backoffice -> Content -> Content Manager -> Hero -> Add a new slide -> Add background image 
-> Check 'Additional Information'-> Add Title image 
```

{% endcode %}

### Option 3: Branded Hero Image, Channel logo&#x20;

Add a sponsorship logo to a hero image as a Channel Logo. Unlike the title image logo in the above use case, channel logo can be placed above the title image png logo. Channel logo is beneficial if you have pre-existing content or events that you would like to be 'presented by' a sponsor.&#x20;

E.g. The Endeavor Streaming logo placed above "The Night Manager" title image.&#x20;

### Option 4: Branded Hero by Geography&#x20;

Activate local market sponsors with geographic segmentation. If you have a global service spanning multiple territories, leverage our segmentation tool to upload different hero imagery based on territory.

E.g. you may have a sponsorship just for your US based event. If so, you may want to create a sponsorship hero just for the US audience.&#x20;

[Here's how ](https://docs.onvesper.com/platform-knowledge-base/back-office/content/content-management/segmentation#segment-by-geography)you can segment hero by geography.&#x20;

### Branded Sections

Create a dedicated branded [section](/platform-knowledge-base/back-office/content/content-management/sections.md) for the ultimate real-estate for your sponsorship partner. You may create a new section and curate that page in exactly the same way as the Home & Browse page, allowing for deeper content nesting and sub categorization of content libraries for your sponsorship.&#x20;

Leverage Vesper's full suite of content management tooling to build this sponsorship section. Make the section include:

* Sponsorship branded hero images
* Sponsorship branded content rows (VODs, playlists, live events)
* Sponsorship branded live channels + EPGs&#x20;

Once you create the sponsorship section, you may share the link to end users or link out within your homepage *section* content row, or via hero image CTA buttons.&#x20;

E.g. [www.mydomain.com/section/sponsorshipname\&#x20](http://www.mydomain.com/section/sponsorshipname\&#x20);

### Branded Content Thumbnails

Integrate your sponsorship partner's logo into your thumbnail artwork. If you have VODs or live events with your sponsor, bake in the sponsorship logo within your thumbnail artwork.&#x20;

Once you create thumbnails with the sponsorship logo added, use those thumbnails for:

* VODs
* Playlists (season & series)
* Live events&#x20;

You may also create dedicated content rows of your sponsorship VODs, playlists, and live events. That way there is a centralized location within your homepage with the sponsorship events/content that end users can navigate to.&#x20;

### Branded Featured Content Rows&#x20;

Vesper Backoffice allows you to create 'featured' content rows for VODs or playlists. They are designed to draw attention to specific content rows within the UI as a user scrolls through. They highlight the content row by allowing you to insert a background image behind the content row.&#x20;

You can create [featured rows in several ways](/platform-knowledge-base/back-office/content/content-management/creating-rows.md).

E.g. Below is an example of what a **Featured Playlist** can look like. The background image of the woman can be replaced with a sponsorship image.&#x20;

### Branded Live Channels&#x20;

Add a sponsorship logo for your live steam that is sponsored. There are two options for adding a sponsorship logo to a live stream.

* Logo to be baked into the stream on your end&#x20;
* If you cannot add a logo to a stream, you may reach out to your Account Management team to discuss adding a logo overlay to your live streams &#x20;
  * \*\*If you choose this route, note that the logo will apply to **all live streams.** To remove, please reach out to your Account Management team or Support&#x20;

### Branded VOD Assets&#x20;

Vesper allows you to add a sponsorship logo to any individual VOD asset. These sponsorship logos are displayed over the VOD asset either on the top right or top left. Follow the directions [here ](/platform-knowledge-base/dve/video/sponsorship-watermarking.md)on how to configure branded VOD assets.&#x20;

### Branded Promo Notifications&#x20;

Promo notifications allows additional messaging capabilities by displaying a promotional message on the web and mobile application for *logged-in users*.&#x20;

Create a branded pop-up notification that immediately captures user attention for any special offers, ticket sales, or promote sponsorship live events and content.&#x20;

You can create a promo notification by following the directions[ here](/platform-knowledge-base/back-office/content/notifications.md).&#x20;

## Mobile Application

Incorporate a second logo of your sponsorship partner on your mobile app.

### Branded Splash Screen

The splash screen is the initial image/screen displayed when an app is launched on a mobile or TV device. By default, it is used to showcases your brand logo. But in addition to that, you can customize it to include a secondary sponsorship logo (360x225px), as demonstrated in the following example:

<figure><img src="/files/QmmGFzlFkscmjDqOiWlo" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Please reach out to your Account Management team to update your splash screen.

## Reporting

Please note that adding sponsorship logos to your creatives will not generate impressions as this functionality is not utilizing advertising technologies.

## Advertising&#x20;

The Vesper platform can be configured to provide linear advertising for VOD and Live videos. You may use [CSAI pre rolls](/platform-knowledge-base/advertising/advertising-on-vesper.md) to run sponsorship pre roll videos on certain videos. If you are looking to enable ads, please reach out to your Account Management team.&#x20;
