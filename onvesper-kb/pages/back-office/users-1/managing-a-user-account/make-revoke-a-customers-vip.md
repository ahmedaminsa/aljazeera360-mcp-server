# Make/Revoke a Customers VIP

VIP status gives your customers access to all content available on your platform, without the need to purchase a license.

To make a customer a VIP, you need to go to the ‘Customers’ section in Back Office and search for the customer via Username, Name, Transaction ID, Card Number, EXID, Address-Line, or Postal Code.

<figure><img src="/files/IJ2zsLBcTzbfa7PkdPbr" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Select the customer’s name and you will be taken through to the account page.&#x20;

On the right-hand side of the page, you will see the ‘Actions’ button, once selected you will see options drop-down menu where you can select the ‘Make VIP’ option.

<figure><img src="/files/dxCt8xAXE4pNtcyvbifk" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

On the right-hand side corner of the screen, a notification will pop up that will confirm that the customer has been made a VIP. You will also see a VIP marker under the name of the customer.&#x20;

<figure><img src="/files/YuQj2c3GFsKXOdJbEMe4" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### REVOKE A CUSTOMER VIP <a href="#revoke-a-customer-vip" id="revoke-a-customer-vip"></a>

If you need to revoke a customer’s VIP status then this is easy to do, you just need to follow the steps above however when it comes to the ‘Actions’ drop-down menu select ‘Remove VIP’ option which removes the VIP status from the account.

<figure><img src="/files/1URKycBys8zLPMCc7pOB" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

On the right-hand side corner of the screen, you will notice a notification will pop up that will confirm that the customer status has been changed. The VIP marker will also be removed from the profile.&#x20;
