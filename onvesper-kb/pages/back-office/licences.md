# Licences & Promotions

The ‘Licence’ section of Back Office is where the licences you can find the licences that had been created during the on-boarding process. In this section you will also find promotions which gives you the ability to create promotions for your subscribers.

[Promotions](/platform-knowledge-base/back-office/licences/promotions.md): This is where you are able to create promotions which can be offered to your subscription base.
