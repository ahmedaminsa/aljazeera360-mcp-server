# Marketing Partner Management

{% hint style="info" %}
To enable this feature, please reach out to your Commercial Account Manager.
{% endhint %}

## Introduction

This feature allows you to create unique referral partner identities to track the signup and/or purchase conversion effectiveness of individual campaigns you run with each of those partners.

These conversions are tracked **on TV and the web** and occur as an outcome of driving an end-user through a specific marketing/sales channel, inclusive of, but not limited to:

* online advertising
* email marketing
* end-user referral
* Programmatic Demand Side Platforms (DSPs)
* TV app stores

Additionally, for web-based campaigns, you can provide a [post-back URL](https://app.gitbook.com/o/-Lu4VWsQtr5guXJ4j8MW/s/-LvKlMqUAgpPfEFkrjiJ/~/changes/244/back-office/analytics-1/marketing-partner-management/post-back-url) to enable Endeavor to programmatically update the marketing/referral partner systems with conversion event data such that campaign delivery can be optimised.

You can create as many campaigns as you wish!

## Example

You are running an e-mail marketing campaign to acquire new customers with a partner, “Marketing Team X”, and you create a sign-up link which is sent out as part of that campaign. When a user completes a conversion event (a **sign-up**, a **purchase**, or both – you choose), they will then become associated with that specific campaign ID and referral partner.

Customer reports generated by the platform will include the partner who onboarded that customer, as well as which specific campaign they responded to.

## Key Components

The Marketing Partner Management feature contains the following components:

| Component               | Details                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          | Reporting Field          |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Partner                 | <p>Name of the referral partner or sales channel, to be used for reporting and management. </p><p></p><p>Can have a 1:N (one to many) campaign associated.</p>                                                                                                                   | REFERRAL\_PARTNER\_NAME  |
| Campaign                | <p>Unique campaign you wish to track distinct conversion events against.</p><p></p><p>Associated to a unique referral ID.</p><p></p><p>Associated to a single referral partner.</p>                                                                                              | REFERRAL\_CAMPAIGN\_NAME |
| Referral ID             | <p>Unique ID generated by Endeavor Streaming that is a 1:1 (one to one) with a given campaign. </p><p></p><p>Mandatory implementation on to the redirect URL to initiate a marketing session and enable conversion tracking and reporting.</p>                                   | REFERRAL\_ID             |
| Conversion              | The available conversion event types are: user sign-up or purchase.                                                                                                                                                                                                              | CONVERSION\_TYPE         |
| Click ID **(optional)** | <p>Optional parameter associated to a given campaign that can be used to capture additional data on the redirect. </p><p></p><p>Used to pass in your marketing partner's unique identifier, which is then leveraged in the construction of the post-back URL. </p><p></p><p></p> | CLICK\_ID                |

## Conversion reporting

The key components will be reflected in all analytics and reports delivered through [Vesper Insights](/platform-knowledge-base/back-office/vesper-insights.md).

Users who you acquire, or who purchase through partner links will continue to be attributable back to the key components throughout their lifecycle as a customer. Meaning you are able to analyse the long-term stickiness and watch habits of customers acquired through one partner, and compare it to another partner (or campaign).
