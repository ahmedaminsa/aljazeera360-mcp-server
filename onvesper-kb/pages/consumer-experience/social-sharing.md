# Social Sharing

Sharable links can be generated for the following content types:

* VOD
* Live Events
* Live Linear Channels
* Virtual Linear Channels
* Playlists

Once configured, a share icon will be shown.

When clicking on the share icon on the web, the URL is copied to the end-user's clipboard to paste as needed.

For mobile applications, clicking on the share icon brings up options to share VODs, Live Events and Playlists via various installed messaging and social media applications.

<figure><img src="/files/ZKebvfS08P40gVelbrDd" alt="" width="188"><figcaption><p>Native sharing pop-up</p></figcaption></figure>

These links, once shared, will present with metadata showing the appropriate Thumbnail, Title and Description.

<figure><img src="/files/31PriDOaff5haqurGhV8" alt="" width="188"><figcaption><p>Shared link showing Metadata</p></figcaption></figure>

This feature works best with [***Universal links***](/platform-knowledge-base/consumer-experience/mobile/linking-directly-to-content-in-mobile-apps/universal-links.md), enabling mobile operating systems (iOS and Android) to open associated content/links directly in the mobile application, rather than launching in a browser. If you're interested in enabling [Universal Links](/platform-knowledge-base/consumer-experience/mobile/linking-directly-to-content-in-mobile-apps/universal-links.md), speak to your account management team.

### Tracking social share conversion

Vesper can support appending additional URL parameters (?this=something) to the socially shared URLs which are generated by the platform. One set of parameters can be added for all links which are generated.

For example, ***UTM** (Google Analytics)* or ***Referral Ref IDs** (Vesper)* can also be automatically appended to Social Sharing links to track the number of clicks (Google Analytics) or conversions ([Vesper MPM](/platform-knowledge-base/back-office/administration/marketing-partner-management.md)) generated by social sharing. These solutions can be requested by contacting the helpdesk or your account management team.
