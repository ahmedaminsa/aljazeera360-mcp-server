# Understanding Service Emails

The below page outlines the various service emails that we have available on our platform today.

It's important to note that these emails are automatically sent based on certain triggers actioned by the user. These emails are NOT marketing emails and are purely transactional. It is recommended to customise the footer and contact addresses on the e-mails you are using, we can also accept copy edits if required.&#x20;
